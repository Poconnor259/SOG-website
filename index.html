MinionMates

Your tiny companions need you. Care for them, watch them grow.

Tend to the Wellbeing of Your Little Mates

MinionMates is a charming mobile pet-care game where you are responsible for the health, happiness, and overall well-being of a diverse cast of adorable creatures. From feeding and bathing to playing and studying, every action matters!

  • Beautifully designed, changing backgrounds.
  • Dozens of unique MinionMates to collect and bond with.
  • Intuitive interface for tracking Hunger, Energy, and Happiness.
